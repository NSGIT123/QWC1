Monitor Your Water

Monitor the pH, turbidity, temperature, total dissolved solids, conductivity, and salinity of your water, and through our Machine Learning technology, know if your water is safe to drink or not.

Before clicking the button below make sure to...
1. Connect your computer with your Quick Clean Water device using the provided USB.
2. Insert your Quick Clean Water device into your water and wait for 2 1/2 minutes while readings are taken.
3. You will be redirected to the Monitor Your Water page after this time.
Take Contaminant Test

If our system is reporting that your water is non-potable, take this contaminant quiz to narrow down the impurity in your water using Quick Clean Water's Machine Learning technology. Send the test via email to your community as well.
