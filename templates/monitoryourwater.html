pH
Water with a pH level between 6.5 and 8.5 is considered to be drinkable water. Anything below 6.5 is acidic, and anything above 8.5 is alkaline.
Turbidity
Water with a turbidity reading between 0 NTU and 5 NTU is considered to be drinkable water. Any reading above 5 is not acceptable.

Total Dissolved Solids
Water with a TDS reading between 50 ppm and 500 ppm is considered to be drinkable water. Any reading below 50 ppm and above 500 ppm is not acceptable.
Conductivity
Water with a conductivity reading between 0 μS/cm and 1500 μS/cm is considered to be safe water. Any reading above 1500 μS/cm is not safe water.
Salinity
Water with a conductivity reading between 0 mg/L and 900 mg/L is considered to be potable water. Any reading above 900 mg/L is not non-potable water.
